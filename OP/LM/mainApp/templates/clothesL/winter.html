Cold weather will come faster than we think, so it's time to get acquainted with the main trends of the season autumn-winter 2019/2020.

Havelock

Next autumn is to replenish the wardrobe is not just a new trench coat, and a model with a small Cape. In a similar recently in Ireland emerged Duchess Catherine. They call this kind of outerwear "Havelock" in honor of General Henry Havelock, who often wore such models. In the new collections, designers sew trench coats with Cape-capes made of wool, cotton and leather. Especially impressive look havelocks with contrasting capes-in color or texture.



Black Skin

Put on the skin from head to toe, and why not? This is what designers on both sides of the Atlantic offer, and without collusion they choose the most brutal version of this trend â€“ in black. For example, Tom Ford is nostalgic for the daring 2000s and sews pantsuits made of soft leather, on the catwalk at Sally LaPointe, leather overalls in the manner of aviators became the main ones, and Salvatore Ferragamo finally offer to buy a black leather dress.



Strong shoulder

Shoulders again became a focus of attention among designers. Moreover, if last season they mainly emphasized the shoulders on jackets, this time they did it on everyday dresses with the help of sculptural frills, and on elegant tops, thanks to three-dimensional applications, and on evening images, complemented by sleeves made of ruffles.



Red and black cage

The cage continues to play an important role in the fashion agenda. This time her red-and-black combination came into focus. Moreover, the type of the picture is not so important. Equally well against the background of fallen leaves will look and a little brazen cage-Buffalo, which is also called a lumberjack print, and more intelligent tartan. For inspiration, you should look to the collections of Christian Dior, Redemption and Marine Serre.



Seventies

Designers juggle fashion decades of the past constantly. This season, for example, they're back to the seventies, a little tired of the 1980s audacity that took over the catwalks a couple of years ago. The decade of freedom, as the 1970s were called, appeared in all its diversity-bourgeois Paris at Celine, dancing until the morning as in Studio 54 at Michael Kors and Halpern, and Bohemian relaxation again pleased fans of Etro.



Lime flavored

Neon somehow appeared in the collections of the season autumn-winter 2019/2020. Especially many of it was in lime color. Cheerfulness in restrained autumn colors decided to add brands Balenciaga, Nina Ricci and Issey Miyake, which both offer to wear it solo, and combining with classic black or rich purple.
Punks in the city

The spirit of rebellion from the streets of Paris rallying logically moved to the podium. Fashion brands amicably remembered punk, adding to their collections aggressive elements in the form of spikes, a combination of blood red and gray, heavy shoes and a variety of chains. At the same time, designers managed to fit punk into the aesthetics of their brands quite organically-Donatella Versace with its help reduced the traditional level of glamour and sex appeal, and Alexander Wang crossed it with his favorite sports silhouettes.
For each day

The fall-winter 2019/2020 show season will be remembered for the fact that designers finally remembered that women need not only clothes for parties, dinner parties and intergalactic flights, but also for every day. One of the main adherents of building a competent everyday wardrobe was Victoria Beckham, whose new collection is an excellent practical guide. Also, brands like Gabriela Hearst and Lemaire continue to make clear clothes that are easy to live in.
Festive down jacket

Down jackets next fall and winter will turn into a full-fledged item of evening wardrobe. The perfect version of this trend can be seen in the new collection of Pierpaolo Picciolli, who sewed for Moncler luxurious quilted dresses to the floor. But a more realistic adaptation of this story is perfectly shown in Brandon Maxwell and Cristiano Siriano. Could not pass by down jackets and in the French fashion House Hermes. However, here the down jacket is hidden under a seemingly elegant leather jacket. But this is only at first glance.
